Understanding the function of each page in the navigation bar

  • Studies: to REVIEW the inventory by a specific study

  • Check-In Kits: to ADD kits into the inventory

  • Check-Out Kits: to REMOVE kits from the inventory

  • Reports: to REVIEW reports about all studies

Understanding how to add kits to the inventory

  1. Go to the 'Check-In Kits' page
  2. Click on the 'Add Kit Type' button that will allow you to add a type of kit for a specific study
